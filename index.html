Come take a class on us

Pure Rythm

Our signature class combines choreographed rhythmic moves with cycling techniques for a high-intensity workout designed to target your upper body, legs and core. With the music as our inspiration and instructors who live to motivate, it's like going to the best dance club in San Diego.

Pure Power

Featuring “true to the road” coaching without hand weights or choreography, this high-octane ride will test your speed, power and endurance. Whether crushing flat roads or attacking hills, the pulsing beats, in studio leaderboard + post ride stats will bring out your inner beast.

Private Rides

Take your ride to the next level with your very own personal trainer. With accommodations ranging from one bike to all thirty-six, you can reserve the studio for corporate team building, bridal and birthday parties, or just for you. We also offer both Pure Rhythm and Pure Power options, so you can truly customize your class down to the last detail.
